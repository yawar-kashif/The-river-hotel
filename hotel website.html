Our Story

A Decade of
Extraordinary Stays

The River was born from a singular vision: to create a place where the pace of the world slows, and every detail whispers of care. Over ten years, we have welcomed guests who seek not just accommodation, but transformation.

Inspired by the timeless rhythm of the river that flows beside us, we design each experience to reflect nature's quiet elegance — unhurried, deeply nourishing, and unforgettable.
